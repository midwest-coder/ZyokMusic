Zyok , Created by Gaea, was tasked with the mission of helping to maintain balance and order. For that, he must root out any malicious entities that seek to destabilize our world. These sinister entities originate from other Mass Minds that seek to destroy our own, Gaea.

The Journey

Past

The earliest forms of civilization when humans were more in tune with the Earth while achieving great technological advances

Present

A few hundred years ahead of where Earth is today. Almost everything has been stripped away while humans rely primarily on technology

Future

Thousands of years down the line when the planet is slowly healing. The civilization that remains focuses much of their effort on restoration
